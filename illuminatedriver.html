Illuminated River

Fancy seeing a river light up with the light from a thousand fairies? Then come on down to the Thames Bridges in Central London, to see Illuminated River, in hour of the historical architecture

It is the world's largest public art commission, as it is 3.2 miles from London to Lambeth Bridges. Also, it is expected to last for at least a decade, where the fountain will cover all service and energy expenditures

The delicately flowing streaks of LED lights in the river reflect the grandeur of the current architecture of London's bridges and their connection to the river that runs below them.

When?

From dusk till dawn

London Bridge, Cannon Street, Southwark and Millenium Bridge outside Tate Modern- will be turned off at 2am

Where?

Bridge Locations: London Bridge, Cannon Street Railway Bridge, Southwark Bridge, Millenium Bridge, Blackfriars Bridge, Waterloo Bridge, Golden Jubilee Footbridges, Westminster Bridge, Lambeth Bridge, Albert Bridge, Chelsea Bridge, Grosvenor Railway Bridge, Vauxhall Bridge and Tower Bridge
