Wave

Greenwich peninsula

Greenwich Peninsula presents Wave, an interactive sculptural light installation by British experimental lighting designers, Squidsoud.

From 29th November, a dynamic audio-visual experience, Wave, creates an entrancing physical presence of how light, sound and movement can work in perfect harmony. The five hundred part voice and light experience in the form of a sculptural wave, consists of some five hundred individual suspended orbs, offering the most unusual displays of festive light experiences this season.

Situated in Peninsula Square, the 20-metre sculpture will frame the entrance to the new Design District creating a dynamic illuminated wave that weaves between NOW Gallery and new food hub Canteen

Please handle the work with care. All children should be accompanied by an adult. Standard entry: Free, queues expected at busy periods Nearest Tube: North Greenwich (Jubilee Line)
