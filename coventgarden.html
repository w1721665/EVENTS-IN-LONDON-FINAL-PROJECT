Covent Garden

Missing snow in London? Not to worry! Covent Garden is creating an experience of having a White Christmas.

Covent Garden will be sprinkled with snow every day, alongside its spectacle displays of over 115,000 lights, giant mistle-toe chandeliers, London's largest hand-picked tree and much more!

The new sprinkling snow addition will create a fairytale for all its visitors.

Every hour, midday-7pm
