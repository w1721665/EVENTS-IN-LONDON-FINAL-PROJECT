Natural History Museum Ice Rink

Enjoy a day at this fun venue with all your loved-ones in this winter season

With a backdrop of the Waterhouse building, fairy-lit frost covered trees encompassing the rink, prepare to have a magical skating experience.

When?

OPEN DAYS: 22nd October 2021- 16th January 2022

Opening Times

Open 11.00-22.00 daily (times may vary)

Where?

Location: Cromwell Road, London SW7 5BD

How to get tickets:

Book your skate session online to guarantee your place or buy your tickets from the box office when you arrive.

Remember, you may have to wait for your turn on the ice if you haven't booked your session in advance, especially during peak times.

ENTRY INFORMATION:

  • Please dress appropriately
  • Keep children under the age of 15 supervised
  • Toilets will be provided for attendees on site
  • Any pets are not permitted on the ice skating rink
